## Overview

RedTape simplifies legal processes using AI.

Users can upload contracts, ask questions about legal documents, identify risky clauses, generate legal notices, and find suitable lawyers through an intelligent recommendation system.

The goal is to make legal services more accessible, understandable, and affordable.

---

## Features

### Contract Analyzer
- Upload legal documents
- Clause extraction
- Risk identification
- Highlight important sections

### AI Legal Assistant
- Ask questions about uploaded documents
- Context-aware responses
- Legal information retrieval

### Notice Generator
- Generate legal notices automatically
- Multiple notice templates
- Export-ready documents

### Lawyer Recommendation
- Match users with lawyers
- Practice-area filtering
- Lawyer dashboard support

### OCR & PDF Processing
- Extract text from scanned documents
- PDF highlighting
- Document conversion

### Legal Learning Hub
- Educational resources
- Legal awareness content
- Guided learning modules
